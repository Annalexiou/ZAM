Does stress and pressure overtake your daily life?

As we all know most people in our planet struggle everyday with anxiety... And since this is a worldwide issue, concerning mainly teenagers and young adults, it is a must that we provide useful information in order to help.

So here are some ways to help reduce stress and anxiety.

1.Meditation


According to the Humanity University, meditation helps to train your mind and help you to focus and redirect your thoughts. It helps to increase the awareness of your surroundings and yourself. Meditation is also used by a lot of people to create other beneficial feelings and habits!


Here are some easy steps to help you start meditating

First you must find a quiet and comfortable space. This should be a place where you can relax without being interrupted or distracted. Then you should sit comfortably. You can use a chair, a cushion, or even your bed. It is important to maintain good posture without feeling strained. At last close your eyes and take deep breaths whilst paying attention to the movement of your breath and let your thoughts flow freely.
After doing this for a few minutes you will start feeling a sesne of calm and relaxation.

2. Exerxice


Working out regularly is one of the best ways to relax your body and mind.During exercise and other physical activities, your body produces endorphins. These are natural chemicals in the brain that help relieve pain, reduce stress, help you sleep and improve your sense of wellbeing.


These are some exersices that you could do:

    Go out for a walk or run

    Take a bike ride

    Do a workout at home

    Do some physical housework or gardening

    Visit a free outdoor gym

    Sign up to a sports team.
